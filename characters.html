Characters

Meet the memorable characters

Main Characters

Walter White

A chemistry teacher who turns to cooking meth after being diagnosed with cancer. He transforms into the ruthless drug lord Heisenberg. His transformation from a sympathetic family man to a dangerous criminal is the central arc of the series.

Jesse Pinkman

Walter's former student and meth-cooking partner. Despite his criminal life, he maintains a moral conscience and loyalty to Walter. His journey explores themes of redemption and the consequences of one's choices.

Skyler White

Walter's wife who discovers his meth empire and struggles to protect her family while dealing with the moral implications. She becomes an unwilling accomplice in his criminal activities.

Hank Schrader

Walter's brother-in-law, a DEA agent who investigates the meth trade and gets close to discovering Walter's secret. His pursuit of Heisenberg creates tension throughout the series.

Saul Goodman

A lawyer who specializes in helping criminals. He becomes Walter and Jesse's legal advisor and fixer. His later spin-off series Better Call Saul explores his backstory.

Mike Ehrmantraut

A former police officer turned hitman and private security. He works for Gus Fring and later becomes an ally to Walter and Jesse. His moral code and professionalism make him a memorable character.
