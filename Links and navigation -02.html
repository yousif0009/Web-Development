Overview

Breaking Bad is an American neo-Western crime drama television series created and produced by Vince Gilligan. The show follows Walter White, a high school chemistry teacher diagnosed with inoperable lung cancer who turns to manufacturing and selling methamphetamine to secure his family's financial future.

Alongside his former student Jesse Pinkman, Walter enters the dangerous world of drug trafficking, evolving from a sympathetic family man to a ruthless criminal mastermind known as "Heisenberg."

Main Characters

Walter White

A chemistry teacher who turns to cooking meth after being diagnosed with cancer. He transforms into the ruthless drug lord Heisenberg.

Jesse Pinkman

Walter's former student and meth-cooking partner. Despite his criminal life, he maintains a moral conscience and loyalty to Walter.

Skyler White

Walter's wife who discovers his meth empire and struggles to protect her family while dealing with the moral implications.

Hank Schrader

Walter's brother-in-law, a DEA agent who investigates the meth trade and gets close to discovering Walter's secret.

Saul Goodman

A lawyer who specializes in helping criminals. He becomes Walter and Jesse's legal advisor and fixer.
